How to Get Your LinkedIn PDF

  1. Go to your LinkedIn profile and click the "More" button below your name.
  2. Select "Save to PDF" from the dropdown menu.
  3. Upload the downloaded file here.

Tip: On mobile, you may need to use "Desktop site" mode in your browser to see this option.

For the Best Results

  • Use a Job Description: Pasting a full JD gives our AI the most context to tailor your profile.
  • Be Specific: A target role like "Software Engineer at a fintech startup" is better than just "Software Engineer".
  • Add Extra Details: Use the next step to add missing projects or skills for a more complete analysis.
